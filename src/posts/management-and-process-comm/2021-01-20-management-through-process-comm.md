So let's face it : I've done my process communication test and that was very interesting. Why is that ? Because I've discovered that in addition to having changed phases several times, I now had at my disposal a very complete tool to help me manage.

# So what is the process communication ?

Created by Taibi Kahler's research and used by the N.A.S.A, the Process Communication Model has made his apperance in the 80's in France. PCM is a quite reliable, behaviourally based communication model, used to individually tailor connection and motivation and build trust and rapport. The PCM profile provides to a person insights about how he/she percives the world, how they communicate, how they are motivated and how the behave under stress. PCM answers the "So what? Now what" questions about how to communicate with different types of people. 

PCM is useful not only for an individual but also for a company. PCM will ease communication by offering more flexibility, more adaptability and will help open your mind. For a company PCM is useful for management, taking position, speaking publicly ...
